I am a PhD candidate in CS @ UIUC. My advisor is Prof. Jimeng Sun. I also closely work with Dr. Cao (Danica) Xiao from Amplitude and Prof. M. Brandon Westover from Harvard Medical School. I finished B.S. in Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) from 2015 to 2019.

I am working on addressing real-world healthcare problems with deep learning methods, such as self-supervised learning, tensor decomposition and graph neural networks. Previously, I worked with Prof. Tarek Abdelzaher, Prof. Hari Sundaram, Prof. Xiaofeng Gao and Prof. Gongshen Liu.
