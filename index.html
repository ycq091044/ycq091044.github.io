I am a PhD student in CS @ UIUC. My advisor is Prof. Jimeng Sun. I finished B.S. in Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) from 2015 to 2019.

I am working on event sequence modeling (for healthcare problems). My previous works include self-supervised learning, tensor decomposition, graph neural network, social polarization mining, RTB, event popularity prediction, etc. Previously, I worked with Prof. Tarek Abdelzaher, Prof. Xiaofeng Gao and Prof. Gongshen Liu.
