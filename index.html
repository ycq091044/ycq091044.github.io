I am a 4th-year PhD candidate in CS @ UIUC. My advisor is Prof. Jimeng Sun. I also closely work with Dr. Cao (Danica) Xiao from Relativity and Prof. M. Brandon Westover from MGH Sleep Lab and Harvard Medical School. Previously, I worked with Prof. Tarek Abdelzaher, Prof. Hari Sundaram, Prof. Xiaofeng Gao and Prof. Gongshen Liu. I am working on building machine learning models and systems for signals and sequence data, mostly in the healthcare domain. I welcome any potential research or open-source collaborations!
